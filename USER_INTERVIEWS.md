## Interview 1 — A.K. — Startup Founder — 12-person SaaS startup
Direct Quotes
“We keep paying for tools nobody remembers subscribing to.”
“AI costs exploded faster than our cloud bill this quarter.”
“I don't want another dashboard unless it saves money immediately.”
Most Surprising Thing

The founder cared more about unused subscriptions than pricing optimization.

What It Changed

I added clearer unused-seat recommendations and simplified the dashboard summary.

## Interview 2 — S.P. — Indie Hacker — Solo founder
Direct Quotes
“I constantly test AI tools and forget to cancel trials.”
“A lightweight audit email every month would actually help.”
“Most SaaS dashboards are too enterprise-focused.”
Most Surprising Thing

A solo founder preferred email-based reports over a complex dashboard.

What It Changed

I started considering lightweight recurring summaries instead of dashboard-only experiences.
