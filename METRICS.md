## Completed Audits Per Week

This metric best represents actual user value because the product only becomes useful when a full audit is generated.

### Three input metrics driving the North Star:

Landing page conversion rate
Audit completion rate
Returning users generating follow-up audits

The first instrumentation priorities would be:

Drop-off points in the audit flow
Most selected AI tools
Average estimated savings per audit
Time required to complete an audit

A pivot decision would trigger if:

Fewer than 10% of users complete audits after signup
Users consistently ignore recommendations
Consultation conversion remains below 2%

Those signals would indicate weak product-market fit or poor onboarding clarity.
