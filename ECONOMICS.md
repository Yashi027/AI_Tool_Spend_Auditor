Assume Credex sells consulting or AI optimization services after the audit. A converted lead could realistically be worth between $2,000 and $10,000 depending on company size and consulting scope.

For estimation purposes:

Average consulting deal: $4,000
Gross margin: 70%
Effective profit per converted customer: $2,800

If the audit tool converts qualified leads efficiently, even a relatively small number of conversions could justify acquisition efforts.

Estimated CAC by channel:

Founder community outreach: ~$10 CAC
Product Hunt launch: ~$30 CAC
Organic X/LinkedIn content: ~$15 CAC
Referral partnerships: ~$20 CAC

Suppose:

100 users complete audits
15% book a consultation
20% of consultations convert

That produces:

100 audits → 15 consultations → 3 paying customers

At $4,000 average revenue:

3 × $4,000 = $12,000 revenue

Even with operational costs, this becomes profitable quickly because acquisition costs remain relatively low.

To reach $1M ARR in 18 months:

Need approximately $83k MRR
At $4k/customer average revenue, roughly 21 new consulting-equivalent deals monthly
Alternatively, launch a SaaS subscription tier:
2,000 customers paying $49/month = ~$98k MRR

For that to happen, the platform would need:

Reliable automated audits
Strong referral loops
Integrations with billing platforms
Founder trust through public case studies
Low-friction onboarding
